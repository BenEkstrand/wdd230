Bronze

  • All non-profit benefits plus:
  • Minor spell of Enchantment applied to Business listing.
  • 25% discount on Hex removal, curse-breaking, minor health elixirs.

Silver

  • All bronze benefits plus:
  • 50% on Hex removal, curse-breaking, minor health elixirs.
  • Phantasmal projection of Business upon gazing at posted advertisement.
  • Special event banners at all gladiatorial combat arenas.
  • Monthly personal visit from the sitting Council on business matters.

Gold

  • All silver benefits plus:
  • Free admission to Council hosted events.
  • Free reanimation/resurrection for up to 4 employees and 50% off for all others on Hex removal, curse-breaking, minor health elixirs.
  • Personal endorsement of business by reigning gladiatorial champion (while living).
  • Free travel through Translocation Network up to 30 times monthly.
