Website Planning

Target Audience:

Adventures/Heroes

From far and wide the draw for exciting tales of heroism and deeds of valor can be attained in a ruined city that once thrived. Heroes have no end of people that need saving and evil that needs to be vanquished. Even for the neutral among you, there is plenty of ancient and powerful items left to rot by the previous owners that were either slain or lost things in their hasty flight from the destruction. Great power and riches are tantalizing for anyone willing to risk everything and roll the dice to make a name for themselves.

Evil Entities/Villains

Power, power is up for grabs here, raw untamed magics, beasts twisted by corrupted deeds and arcane use gone wild, waiting to be subjugated and thrust back into the world to wreak havoc amongst the unsuspecting populous. Untold power weapon and armor can be obtained from untouched armories and enchanted mage towers to fill the coffers or build an army. All can be had for the small price of rising your end.
